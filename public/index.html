COVID-19

Stay home, stay safe


The Covid-19 pandemic has affected people all over the world and has forced many to change their daily routines in order to stay safe. One of the most effective ways to prevent the spread of the virus is to stay home as much as possible. By staying home, individuals can limit their contact with others, reduce their risk of exposure, and help prevent the virus from spreading to vulnerable populations. Additionally, staying home can also help ease the burden on healthcare systems and essential workers who are on the frontlines of the pandemic.

Take steps to Protect yourself

Wear A Face Mask

Wearing a mask is important as it can help reduce the spread of respiratory droplets that may contain the Covid-19 virus, thereby protecting both the wearer and those around them.

Wash Your Hands

Washing one's hands regularly is important as it can help prevent the transmission of germs and viruses, including Covid-19, and reduce the risk of infection.

Avoid Close Contact

Avoiding close contact with others, especially those who are sick, is important in preventing the spread of Covid-19 as it can reduce the likelihood of transmission.

What Are The Main Symptoms?

Below are some of the listed symptoms one might experience if affected by COVID-19. Please note that some might appear asymptomatic and of varying degrees of the symptoms below

  • Fever
  • Fatigue
  • Dry Cough
  • Sore Throat
  • Aches and Pains
  • Shortness of Breath

Things not to do during Covid

If under circumstances of distress during covid, make sure to not do either of these things:

  • Do Not Share food
  • Avoid touching your face
  • Do Not remain in physical contact with Sick People

Measures to take during covid - affected environment

If under circumstances of distress during covid, consider below steps and measures to keep you and others at safety:

  • Wash your Hands for atleast 20 seconds
  • Wear a Mask at all times
  • Seek Medical Care Regularly
